# KitchenKrew

## Description
KitchenKrew is a pickleball game management platform that makes organizing events effortless for game managers while giving players complete visibility into their matches, partners, and performance. It supports all major game formats—from casual open play to structured tournaments—with an elegant, intuitive interface that requires minimal training.

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Manual game organization is tedious
Automates player rotation, court assignments, and match scheduling across 11+ game formats including Open Play, Round Robin, King of the Court, and single/double elimination tournaments.

### Problem 2: Score tracking is chaotic
Centralized score entry with configurable permissions (GM only, players, or both), optional confirmation workflows, dispute resolution, and complete historical records.

### Problem 3: Players don't know what's happening
Real-time notifications for court assignments, "you're up next" alerts, live game status views, and clear visibility into rotation schedules and upcoming matches.

### Problem 4: No way to track improvement
Comprehensive player statistics including win/loss records, win rates by partner, opponent head-to-head records, rating trends over time, and streak tracking.

### Problem 5: Skill mismatches ruin games
DUPR integration for standardized ratings, skill-based player matching, and configurable assignment rules to ensure balanced, competitive play.

## Key Features
- Multiple game formats (Open Play, Round Robin, King of the Court, Challenge Court, Single/Double Elimination, Pool Play, Ladder, Singles)
- Club and venue management with court tracking and availability
- Event creation with registration, waitlists, and QR code check-in
- Live game status dashboard and configurable score entry
- Player profiles with ratings, preferences, and partner/opponent history
- Leaderboards at event, club, and global levels
- Email and push notifications with player-configurable preferences
- Achievement badges, milestone celebrations, and streak tracking
